Newborns begin gaining weight again at two weeks — and with that weight comes change. Those curled-up, sleepy poses that feel so natural in the first days become harder to achieve as your baby grows stronger and more alert. The best time to book is during your third trimester, so your session is reserved and ready the moment your little one arrives.
